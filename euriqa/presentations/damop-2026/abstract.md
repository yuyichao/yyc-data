Engineering quantum simulation Halmiltonian in a trapped ion quantum computer

Yichao Yu, Vivian Zhang, Bahaa Harraz, Keqin Yan, Debopriyo Biswas, Crystal Noel, Alexander Kozhanov, Christopher R Monroe

Quantum simulator based on trapped ion chains is a natural fit for studying spin dynamics in a 1D system. However, despite the all-to-all connectiveity of two qubit gates in a long ion chain, long-range or higher dimensional conectivity remains challenging when operating in the simulator regime. In this work, we explore different ways to implement more flexible interactions in a ion chain and compare their efficiency and fidelity when implement on certain higher dimensional models.
